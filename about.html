About

Hey again, I'm Adam.

Senior Product Designer based in Orange, California, building thoughtful products across native, web, and AI-enabled experiences.

I'm a Senior Product Designer based in Orange, California, focused on building scalable software for complex, data-driven systems. My work lives at the intersection of UX, engineering, and applied AI, translating technical platforms into intuitive tools that perform under real-world operational constraints.

My background began in analytics and data operations, which shaped how I approach design today: grounded in systems thinking, measurable outcomes, and technical feasibility. Over the years I’ve worked across fintech, gambling, logistics, data analytics, connected-device ecosystems, and large-scale platforms serving irrigation, golf, agriculture, and residential smart-home markets.

I specialize in turning dense workflows into confident, efficient experiences: real-time dashboards, field-tested mobile tools, setup flows for connected hardware, and platform design systems that scale across user types and device categories.

Alongside product design, I invest heavily in applied AI through prototyping tools, model-driven workflows, and new ways of accelerating research, iteration, and engineering collaboration.

My goal is simple: design software that feels clear, reliable, and effortless, even when the underlying systems are anything but.
