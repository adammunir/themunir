Hi again, I'm Adam.

I am a product designer & developer living in Los Angeles. I moved to California in 2018 from England to study for a Masters degree and returned home to continue my career. I moved back in 2021, and I am glad I am 4000miles away from the rain and gloom that London has to offer.

I have worked in a variety of industries from gambling, logistics and fintech to name a few. I started out designing for the marketing department, trying to sharpen my design skills and technical aptitude by learning many of the creative tools and languages I use today.

As someone who mainly focuses on the product side of things, I enjoy being a multidisciplinary designer and working on a wide scope of mediums with a focus on new feature sets and prototyping. At the core I enjoy perfectly crafted products and pixel perfection, while making complex systems easily digestible.

Away from my work life, I enjoy spending time with my family and traveling. I have a passion to learn new things constantly, be it work and tech related or politics, history and science. I am also unfortunately a Lakers fan and that has not going too well at the moment (hope that changes soon!).

If you want to chat, feel free to contact me.
