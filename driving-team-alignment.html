Driving Team Alignment

Collaborative strategy, hands-on execution, and a team-first mindset.

I don’t just give advice — I roll up my sleeves and help teams build products that matter. Working closely with cross-functional teams, I help shape early ideas, sharpen roadmaps, and strengthen product visions with practical, actionable input rooted in real-world experience.
